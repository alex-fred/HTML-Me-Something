My Hobbies

By: Alex Frederick
Published: 2 Days Ago

I have a lot of hobbies and interests that take up most of the space in my mind and my free time. I've always been into making and creating things out of nothing. I love things that are "vintage", durable, and made to last. I have an analog mindset. I'm a classic man.

Hockey

I've played hockey all my life. I grew up in Michigan, which is a hot bed for the sport. I had a wealth of opportunities and I got to play with so many talented and well-known people. I still play hockey to this day, but there it's a lot less competitive.

Teams I've Played For
  • Lapeer Storm
  • Michigan Mountain Cats
  • Lapeer East Eagles

Vinyl Collecting

Music has defined almost every moment in my life; I am always listening to it. When I get the chance to break away from the convenience of digital audio, I step into the world of lo-fi. I have a turntable and hundreds of records that I've been collecting for years. It's a relaxing medium for listening to your favorite songs. It promotes patience and opens the mind to music.

Favorite Albums
  • Frightened Rabbit - Pedestrian Verse
  • Shakey Graves - Roll The Bones
  • Cliff Martinez - Drive Soundtrack

Art

I like to create art. I've had an eye for creativity since I was a kid. I'm always looking at things in a creative aspect. I have a tendency to be extremely calculated and carefully measured in the art that I create, as opposed to fre flowing and abstract.

Favorite Subjects/Medium
  • Pencil
  • Linoleum Carving/Block Printing
  • Oil On Canvas

Music

I play a few musical instruments as well. I started on the drums when I was a kid. When I was in high school I taught myself the guitar and the bass. I write and record my own music.

Instruments I Play/Medium
  • Drums
  • Guitar
  • Mandolin
  • Bass

—It's great to have a creative outlet for your ideas. I believe everyone should invest in discovering and pursuing their hobbies.
